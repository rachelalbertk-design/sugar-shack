Sugar Shack Privacy Policy Draft

This is a release-prep draft. Publish a final hosted version before App Store submission.

Summary

Sugar Shack is designed to work offline. The prototype does not collect personal data, does not use accounts, does not show ads, and does not track players across apps or websites.

Local Save Data

The game stores progress, settings, and completed-intro status on the player's device. This data is used only to resume the game and remember comfort settings.

Data Not Collected

The prototype does not collect location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, health, payment, advertising identifiers, analytics events, or diagnostic logs.

Deleting Data

Players can clear local progress from the in-game Settings panel.
